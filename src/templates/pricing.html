Commonly asked questions

  • How are monthly active users calculated?

    We like to keep things simple, because it makes things well...simple. Every time your boardme script is downloaded by one of your users, we count that as an active user.

  • Do I need a developer to get started?

    No, you can start building onboarding experiences with boardme, without a developer. To activate any guidance live, you will need to implement the boardme code snippet into the codebase of your site, for which you may need a developer.

  • Can I cancel at any time?

    You sure can. Just go to your account settings and click cancel. No need to call our sales team to then be pulled through a guilt trip for wanting to leave. We feel if our product does what we promise, you will never want to cancel. And if you cancel in the middle of a billing cycle you will be refunded the time not used for the rest of that cycle within five to seven business days.

  • Do I need to provide my credit card details when I sign up?

    Nope. Sign up, enjoy our product and when your trial is over we will ask you for your credit card information. We want you to get started with the least amount of doors to walk through.
